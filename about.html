About Sanskar

I’m a Product Specialist at Anakage, helping enterprises scale digital adoption through tailored product demos, solutioning, and hands-on proof-of-concepts (POCs). I work closely with customers to ensure our platform solves real problems—delivering measurable value and business impact.

My responsibilities span across pre-sales, technical enablement, and strategic consultation. I lead product walkthroughs, drive POCs from initiation to closure, handle technical Q&A, and support feasibility analysis and business case development.

Beyond customer-facing work, I contribute to product improvement through structured feedback loops, gap analysis, and market research—helping align product evolution with enterprise needs.

I’m passionate about bridging product and business—reducing friction, improving onboarding, and achieving measurable digital adoption outcomes.
